What is a library Workshop


Library Workshops are a way to improve on your own educational skills with other students as well as a member of the university to lead the session and help guide you on your academic journey. These workshops are one of many ways to gain CPD hours.

Effective Time Managment


This Workshop revolves around the concept of managing time and scheduling everyday tasks to ensure that the student is able to make deadlines and sumbit work in time. These Workshops vary through out the week and are on a mutiple amount of times, so if one session is missed there will always be another one.

Learning from feedback


In this workshop it provided the space to understand and know how to take constructive, positive and negative critisism as well as analysing examples of feedback and creating an action plan to help turn the feeback into something useful that can be taken away from the workshop
