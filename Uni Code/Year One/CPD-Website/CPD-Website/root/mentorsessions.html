What are Mentor Sessions


Mentor Sessions are 30 mintues sessions, once a week, where a student goes into a room and has the oppuruntiy to express any concerns or issues they have relating to the course. In most cases they can be used if a course tutor or personal tutor is reachable by email.

Student Mentors


Student Mentors are 2nd year students who have experience of what it's like to be a NTU student as well as being in a poistion of support.Usally these poistions are taken by students of high standards.

Personal Tutor


Personal Tutor is similar to a Student mentor but in this case they have graduated from university and most likely have PHD in their chosen field. They are responsible for a set of computing students and ensure that their wellbeing is in good shape
